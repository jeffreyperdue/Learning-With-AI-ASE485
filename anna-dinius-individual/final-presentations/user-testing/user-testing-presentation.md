## Other Things I Learned

- The effectiveness of each method depends on the context of the web-app you're testing, including its stage of development and the purpose of conducting the tests.
- In general, the ideal testing strategy is to combine at least 2 different methods.
- For most of the testing methods, a large number of test users is not necessary because the majority (85%) of bugs/errors/problems can usually be found with only 5 test users.
